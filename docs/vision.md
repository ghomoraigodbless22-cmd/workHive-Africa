# WorkHive Africa Vision

WorkHive Africa is a platform that connects people who need services with individuals who can provide those services.

The platform focuses on students, freelancers, artisans, skilled workers, and local laborers across Africa.

Mission:
To reduce unemployment and create economic opportunities by connecting talent with demand.

Target Users:
- Students
- Freelancers
- Electricians
- Plumbers
- Cleaners
- Tutors
- Carpenters
- Mechanics
- Small Businesses
